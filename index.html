Our Story

At Hemp Foundry, our mission is to provide the best quality Hemp products at the most affordable price. Hemp is used in over 25,000 products and as our tagline reads – “We are all things Hemp” – We want to provide Hemp in all fashions of products with the finest quality. We source our hemp from legal industrial hemp sources and from farms that have the highest standards in planting, harvesting, and production. All of our products are manufactured under strict guidelines and fully conform to the Industrial Hemp Research Farm Bill Act of 2014. Under these industrial hemp research programs, America is making a comeback with this versatile crop and we are proud to be at the forefront of this revival. Farmers in many states across America are now growing industrial hemp with federal backing. Hemp Foundry was founded by US veterans and is family owned. Our goal to help bring back the US hemp industry and make it affordable to people looking for the benefits of hemp. This is why it’s important for us to source only from respected hemp growers and purchase our ingredients and materials from US companies. All of the hemp used in our products is lab tested by an independent 3rd party to ensure it is of the highest quality and contains no harmful by-products or chemicals. We are 100% sure that you will be satisfied with our products and service. If for any reason you are not 100% satisfied, simply reach out to us and we will make things right.
